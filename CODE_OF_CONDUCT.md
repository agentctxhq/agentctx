# Code of Conduct

## The Short Version

Be respectful. Be constructive. Focus on the work.

## Expected Behavior

- Communicate with patience and good faith
- Give feedback on ideas and code, not on people
- Welcome contributors regardless of their experience level
- Accept that maintainers have final say on project direction

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language
- Dismissing contributions without constructive reasoning
- Sustained disruption of discussions or the review process

## Enforcement

Violations can be reported by opening a private GitHub issue or contacting
the maintainer directly. Reports will be handled discreetly and fairly.
Consequences range from a warning to a permanent ban depending on severity.

## Scope

This applies to all project spaces: issues, pull requests, discussions, and
any other official communication channels.
